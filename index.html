Programs & Certifications

Choose a path that fits your goals. All programs include exam prep and career coaching.

CNA - Hybrid

Preparation for the Georgia CNA exam with classroom, lab, and clinical hours focused on direct patient care, ADLs, vital signs, and resident rights.

  • Hours: Approx. 45 classroom/lab + 24 clinical
  • Timeline: 4 weeks
  • Tuition: Around $1,100 (plus fees)

CMA ‚Äî Medical Assistant (Comprehensive)

15-week program combining front/back office, phlebotomy, EKG, injections, and practice management with an 80-hour externship.

  • Hours: ~194 total (lecture, lab, externship)
  • Format: In-person or hybrid
  • Tuition: Around $2,900 (plus fees)
Apply View full details

Phlebotomy Technician (CPT)

8-week, 101-hour course in venipuncture, capillary draws, specimen handling, and safety, with a 40-hour clinical externship.

  • Hours: 101 (online, lecture, lab, clinical)
  • Format: In-person or hybrid
  • Tuition: Around $1,100 (plus fees)
Apply View full details

EKG Technician

8-week, 84-hour EKG course covering cardiac anatomy, 12-lead setup, rhythm interpretation, telemetry, and patient care.

  • Hours: 34 lecture, 34 lab, 16 externship
  • Format: In-person or hybrid
  • Tuition: Around $1,100 (plus fees)
Apply View full details

Medication Aide

Short hybrid course for CNAs in good standing to administer routine non-injectable medications safely and accurately.

  • Hours: ~30 hours over ~2 weeks
  • Format: Hybrid
  • Tuition: $500 (plus fees)
Apply View full details

CPR / BLS & RN Prep

AHA-aligned CPR/BLS sessions plus NCLEX-focused RN tutoring in clinical reasoning, dosage calc, and test prep.

  • CPR/BLS: Call to schedule
  • RN Prep: Flexible 1-on-1 or small group
  • Format: In-person tutor-led

Tuition & Financing

Transparent pricing and flexible options so you can focus on learning.

Program Tuition

Most programs use a simple tuition + fees model with optional payment plans.

  • Application fee: typically $75
  • Background check, badge, and insurance as required
  • Certification exam fees paid directly to testing bodies

Payment Plans

Split tuition into multiple payments across your program.

  • Bi-weekly or monthly options
  • One-time financing fee instead of interest
  • Late fees only if payments are missed

Employer & Grants

We can provide documentation for reimbursement or workforce grants.

  • Workforce / WIOA partners
  • Employer sponsorship letters
  • Receipts and transcripts available
